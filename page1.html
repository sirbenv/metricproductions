About Us

BEN VINGER

Ben is from Reno, NV and has always had a creative drive. At a young age he quickly learned to sketch and doodle and later found an outlet through "graffiti street art". He was inspired to make videos when he became heavily involved in snowboarding and skateboarding. "Seeing action sports filmakers bring a unique creative element to film gave me goosebumps. I felt so much emotion when I saw those videos and decided that I wanted to bring my ideas to life and inspire others." 

At the age of 13, Ben co-produced his first short film titled The First Man On The Moon. This was the start to a passionate journey into filmaking. In High School, He was a part of the media productions class and provided journalistic packages to be used in the school news program. He experimented with stop-go animation and claymation in his free time as well. 

Ben is now committed to providing wholesome entertainment in his recent vlog series In The Kitchen With Ben. He aspires to work on larger sets affiliated with The Food Network or The Cooking Channel. He is also passionate about graphic design and is creating short animations which he is hoping will appear on his channel soon. If you would like to know more about Ben please drop him a line here.

MEREDITH BALL

Meredith is an alumnus from the University of Texas at Austin, where she attained her Bachelor of Science in Radio-Television-Film. She became invlolved in theater at a young age and realized that her passion was to entertain others. "I love being a part of an art form where I can make people laugh, dream, and think."

She has had the opportunity to work alongside various creatives such as Scott Rice, Philip Roy, Stan Wong, and Rebecca Campbell. She is known for her work on Fool's Gold, Values, Cruise, Livin' For The Weekend, and Human Traffiking PSA (featuring Matthew McConaughey and the Attorney General).

Her biggest influencers are Martin Scorsese and Alfred Hitchcock; two visionaries that cultivated their own unique approach to filmmaking. If you would like to know more about Meredith please drop her a line here.
